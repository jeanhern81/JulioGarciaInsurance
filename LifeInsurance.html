What are the principal types of life insurance?

  • Term

    • Term Insurance is the simplest form of insurance. It pays only if the death occurs during the term of the policy.

  • Whole Life/Permanent Life

    • Whole Life Insurance covers the person for the duration of their life as long the premiums are paid on time.
